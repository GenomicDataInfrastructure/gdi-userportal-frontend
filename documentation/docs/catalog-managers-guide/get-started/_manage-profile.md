# Manage your profile

Update your profile settings to personalise your catalogue management experience and manage your account preferences.

## Access your profile settings

To access your profile settings:

1. Select the **Profile** icon in the top-right corner of the page.

2. Select **Profile Settings** from the dropdown menu.

## Update your profile information

On the Profile Settings page, you can view and update:

- **Name:** Your display name shown throughout the catalogue
- **Email:** Your contact email address
- **Organisation:** Your affiliated organisation (if applicable)
- **Preferences:** Notification and display preferences

## View your dashboard

Your personal dashboard provides quick access to items you manage:

1. Select the **Profile** icon in the top-right corner.

2. Select **View Dashboard** from the dropdown menu.

Your dashboard displays the following tabs:

- **Newsfeed:** Recent updates and activity in your catalogue
- **My Datasets:** Datasets you have created or manage
- **My Organisations:** Organisations you are associated with
- **My Groups:** Groups you have created or manage
